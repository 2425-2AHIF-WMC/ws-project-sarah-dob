Who I Am

My passion lies in technology and problem-solving, and I’m excited to be learning about programming, systems, and everything that shapes the digital world. Beyond my studies, I enjoy exploring creative hobbies and staying curious about how technology can make our lives better. Being at HTL Leonding allows me to combine my love for innovation with hands-on learning, and I can’t wait to see where this journey takes me.

My Love For Travel

When I’m not diving into coding or school projects, I love traveling with my family. Exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, and seeing the beauty of the world are some of my favorite ways to spend time. Whether it’s visiting bustling cities or relaxing in nature, traveling gives me a fresh perspective and inspires me to dream big. Sharing these adventures with my family makes every trip even more special, and I hope to continue discovering more of the world in the years to come.
